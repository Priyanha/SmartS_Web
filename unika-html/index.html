timeline we achieve

5 AUG 2016


Project Proposal

The project proposal is the main interaction between the client and the product maker. It provides what the system provides, cost and the intial steps to follow when it is made. In the interview panel, it consists of 3 intellectuals who are experted in the fields that are relevant to the functionalities of the system that is proposed.


5 AUG 2016

System Requirement Specification

The SRS presentation is the opportunity that is to interact with the Research Supervisor and show the separate functionalities of the system of the individuals.


5 AUG 2016


Progress Presentation - 1

The progress presentation 01 or Prototype presentation is the situation where the system is at 50% completion. The basic functionalities of the individuals should be working and the whole system is not needed.


5 AUG 2016

Progress Presentation - 2

Progress presentation 02 refers as the situation where the whole system should be working at the level 90% overall. The functionalities should be working together so that the decisions on the final product can be identified by executing the system from start to end.


5 AUG 2016


Final Presentation

Final presentation is the point where the system is fully functional at 100%. Which means that the system must be a full product that is able to commercialize.


5 AUG 2016

Viva

Individual assessment of each member of the group to identify the level of understanding of the system and the functions of the system.
